About This Collection

"Urban Shadows" explores the dramatic interplay between light and shadow in metropolitan environments. Captured over six months across various cities, these monochromatic images reveal the hidden geometry and patterns that emerge when sunlight intersects with urban architecture.

The stark contrast between illuminated surfaces and deep shadows creates a visual language that speaks to the duality of urban existence - highlighting both the structured design of human-made environments and the organic, ever-changing nature of light itself.

Collection Details

  • Captured: January - June 2025
  • Locations: New York, Tokyo, Berlin
  • Equipment: Leica M10 Monochrom
